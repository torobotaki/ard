`Random` in the top menu creates a fresh composition with 1 to 3 drawings.

Each drawing block chooses one original drawing and controls its placement, color, thickness, and audio behavior independently.

`Audio Source` selects the main band for that drawing. `Multiple` unlocks separate routing for size, speed, detail, and color.

`Audio Sensitivity` affects the whole drawing when it is driven by one source. `Mic Sensitivity` affects the incoming signal globally.

`Export` saves the full scene to a file. `Import` restores it later, including theme, colors, and all drawing parameters.

`Theme` changes interface and default palette colors. `M` hides or shows the menu.
