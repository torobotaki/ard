Help

`Start Audio` requests microphone access. `Continue Without Mic` runs in demo mode.

`Space`: next drawing. `R`: weighted random.

`M`: hide or show the menu. If hidden, use the top-right `M` button.

`Config` changes drawing behavior. `Theme` changes the interface and default drawing colors.

`Audio Source` can drive everything together or switch to `Multiple` for separate routing.

Audio Reactive Master

Enable the microphone, then cycle forms.

This sketch uses your mic for motion. `Space` goes to the next form, `R` picks a weighted random one, and `S` hides or shows the interface.

Open this through `http://localhost` or `https` so the browser can expose the microphone.

Show Menu with M or top right M button
